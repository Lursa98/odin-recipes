Homemade Salsa

Description

This is a homemade "salsa roja" the way I learned it in Mexico. This is an authentic spicy salsa on the base of tomato and chilly.

Ingredients

  • Tomato
  • Chilly
  • Lime
  • Celantro
  • Salt
  • Onion

Instructions

  1. Roast the chilly, tomato and onion in the oven until they are charred.
  2. Put everything into a blender with a little bit of lime juice and water.
  3. Add salt and celantro to your liking.
