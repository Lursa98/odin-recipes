Homemade Tortillas

Description

This is a recipe for homemade tortillas. I learned how to make tortillas from scratch the original way when I lived in Mexico. It is very simple, cheap and fast yet very delicious

Ingredients

  • corn flour
  • water

Instructions

  1. Start by slowly mixing the flour with the water, you should start with little water and keep adding until you have a firm dough that has alight bounce to it.
  2. Once you have your dough, take a little portion and form a ball with your hands. The ball should have about golfball size.
  3. Take the ball of dough and press it in your tortilla press to a flat circle.(Optionally you can use anything else to get the same effect) It is recommended to but a piece of parchment paper inbetween so the dough doesn#t stick to the press.
  4. Take the flat dough and but it in your hot pan.
  5. After about 90sec flip.
  6. Repeat this process 2 more times. After the last flip the tortilla should rise and bake in the inside.
